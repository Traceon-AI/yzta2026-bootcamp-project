Lumora acts as the controller of your personal data for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:

- Full name

- Date of birth
- Account credentials

- Mood ratings
- Activity notes

- Usage statistics
- Crash reports

# 3. Why We Process Your Data

We process personal data for the following purposes:

- To create and manage user accounts
- To provide the core functionality of the application
- To personalize wellness recommendations
- To improve application performance through anonymized analytics
- To communicate important service updates
- To detect fraud and protect our services

The legal basis for processing depends on the specific activity.

Where required, processing is based on your consent.

Other processing activities are based on the performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, or our legitimate interests.

You may withdraw your consent at any time through your account settings.

# 4. Sharing Your Data

We never sell your personal data.

We may share limited information with carefully selected service providers, including cloud hosting providers, analytics providers and technical support partners.

All third parties are contractually required to process personal data securely and only on our documented instructions.

---

# 5. International Transfers

Some service providers operate outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

Whenever international transfers occur, we apply appropriate safeguards in accordance with applicable data protection legislation.

# 6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain account information while your account remains active.

Usage analytics data is retained for internal improvement purposes.

privacy@lumora-example.test

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data.

- Encryption of data in transit
- Access control mechanisms
- Authentication procedures
- Regular security reviews
- Continuous monitoring of systems

If a personal data breach occurs, we will promptly investigate the incident and assess the potential impact on affected individuals.

Where required by applicable law, we may notify the competent supervisory authority and affected individuals without undue delay.

If we become aware that personal data has been collected from a child, we will delete that information without undue delay.
